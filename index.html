MORE ABOUT SOUNDLYTICS!!

Soundlytics uses state-of-the-art artificial intelligence (AI) technology to build a sound analysis and enhancement platform. We developed a scalable cloud service to provide speech enhancement and sound analysis services, so users can integrate our technology into their products. Soundlytics provides features such as sound element separation, sound element recognition, voice enhancement, and noise reduction. These features can be widely adopted in the call center industry, especially public safety related ones such as 911 centers. More ...
